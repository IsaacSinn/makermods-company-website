[ MAKERMODS APP · 08 ]

MakerMods App. No code. No Terminal
Just click, teleop, train.

We built MakerMods App so anyone — students, teachers, hobbyists, founders — can drive XLeRobot the moment it's unboxed. A clean, intuitive GUI that wraps the whole LeRobot stack: connect, calibrate, teleop, record datasets, and kick off training runs without writing a single line of code.

  • → One-click pairing over USB. No drivers, no servo addressing.
  • → Drag-to-calibrate. Watch the joints zero in real time.
  • → Bimanual teleop with leader arms. Latency under 12 ms.
  • → Dataset recorder with episode tags, retakes, and previews.
  • → Train on remote GPUs through Qualia — your laptop stays cool.
